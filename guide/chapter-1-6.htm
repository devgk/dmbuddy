Adding content to WordPress website

After WordPress initial setup, you must not sit ideal. You have just deleted all the default content of your WordPress website. Now your WordPress website is completely empty. Now itâ€™s time to start adding new content.

You can add content to your WordPress website by creating page and post.

WordPress Page

It is very similar to normal website page. You can create pages such as about us, contact us etc. Mostly pages contains static contents. There are few points you should know about WordPress page.

  1. Page URL should not be changed.
  2. Page should not be moved or deleted after publishing.
  3. Pages cannot be categorized.
  4. Pages cannot be tagged.
  5. Your website visitors cannot be able to comment on your pages.
  6. Pages should be added to navigation menu.

WordPress Post

A WordPress post is what makes up the blog aspect of your site. These are generally news or informational updates about a certain topic or talking point. Posts are listed in reverse chronological order. There are few points you should know about WordPress post.

  1. Posts can be categorized.
  2. Posts can be tagged.
  3. Posts can be archived.
  4. Posts can be unpublished and republished again.
  5. Your website visitors can comment by default on your posts.
  6. Post should not be added to navigation menu.
  7. Post automatically appears in your blog page.
