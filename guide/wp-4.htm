WordPress initial Setup

Once you have your WordPress blog Installed, you need to make a few changes such as setting up permalinks, updating the ping list, setting up discussion settings, etc. Don’t worry, it’s not technical and you can quickly do it.

Delete Default Post, Page, and Comment

  1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard and go to Posts » All Posts and delete the default “Hello world” post
  2. Add the title of the page, like About, Contact etc.
  3. Similarly, go to Pages » All Pages and delete the default “Sample Page”
  4. And finally, click on “Comments” and delete the default comment

Set Up WordPress Permalinks

  1. Go to Settings » Permalink and select “Post name”
  2. Click “Save”

Set Your Time zone

  1. Go to Settings » General
  2. Set Time Zone to your local time

Remember: Make sure to set your time zone to your local time so that when you schedule posts, they will go live according to your time.

Finalize general Settings

  1. Go to Settings » General
  2. Check your website title and tag line
  3. Check your email address

Enable/Disable User Registration

  1. Go to Settings » General
  2. To allow user registration tick off the “Anyone can register” box

Disable trackbacks and pings

Trackbacks and pingbacks allow blogs to notify each other that they have linked to a post. However, today it is mainly used by spammers to send trackbacks from spam websites. I recommend it to disable this option.

  1. Go to settings » Discussion page
  2. Uncheck the box next to ‘Allow link notifications from other blogs (pingbacks and trackbacks) on new articles’ option
