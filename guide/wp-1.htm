Logging into your WordPress website

After installing WordPress on your hosting server. You need to login to your hosted WordPress website with the username and password which you have set during the installation process.

To login and access the WordPress dashboard in order to begin managing your site, simply type "/wp-admin" at the end of your website URL. Visit “www.your-domain-name.com/wp-admin” to pull up the login screen. Enter your username and password, then click on the Login button.

After successful login, you will be redirected to the WordPress dashboard. In next topic we will discuss the dashboard in details.
