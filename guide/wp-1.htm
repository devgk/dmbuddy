WordPress Guide

In this guide you will learn important settings and options of WordPress. I recommend to start your computer and go through your installed WordPress along with this guide.

Understand WordPress Login

After installing WordPress on your hosting server. You need to login to your WordPress application with the username and password which you have set during the installation process.

To login to your wordpress application type on your browser “Your-domain-name.your-domain-extension/wp-admin”. After successful login, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

WordPress Dashboard

Once you’ve logged in to your WordPress site, you’ll land here, the WordPress Dashboard. WordPress dashboard also known as wp-admin, WordPress backend or WordPress admin panel. It is actually the backend of your WordPress website. Here you will manage, create and update your website. The WordPress Dashboard allows you to control all of the behind-the-scene details of managing your site. Once you find your way around the dashboard, you’ll realize it’s really easy to use and navigate.

Next you will be introduced with options and settings of WordPress dashboard menu.
