WordPress Categories and tags

WordPress Categories and tags are used to organize your WordPress posts in your WordPress website or blog.

By default, WordPress Categories and Tags works only for post.

WordPress categories

WWordPress categories are basic ways for you to organize your posts into different categories, such as topics. Few things you should know about the categories.

  1. Category can be nested into hierarchies. For example, you might have a wildlife category, and under that have children categories such as carnivores, herbivores and omnivores animals.
  2. Category Description is a place for you to add more details about the posts assigned to that category. It is visible on frontend of the website.
