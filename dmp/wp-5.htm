Setting up home page and blog page

Before starting, I recommend you to open your WordPress website. And visit wp-admin “dashboard of your WordPress website”. Login to your WordPress dashboard and follow the instructions.

Set home page

1 The static front page setting can be configured in the Settings » Reading screen. 2 Click on the “A Static Page”. 3 Select your created page as a front page. 4 And click on save

Set Blog Page

1

The blog page setting can be configured in the Settings » Reading screen.

2

“A Static Page” must be checked

3

Select your created “Your Business Blog” page as a post page.

4 And click on save That’s it all done!
