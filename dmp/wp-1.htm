Installing WordPress software on one.com

After purchasing Domain and Hosting Space for your website, now it’s time to setup WordPress on your server.

As you have learned before, WordPress is a CMS. Content management system. It is a website building software. In this topic I will discuss how to install WordPress software on your hosting server, which you have purchased with one.com. If you have purchased hosting server with some other company than please follow their guide to install and setup WordPress. If you are facing any problem then visit DM-Assistance at webenfolds.com.

Now let’s start by logging into your one.com account.

1

Visit one.com and click on control panel button to login into your control panel. Enter your registered email address and password.

Click on Control Panel to login. 2

Once you are logged-in. Click on 1-click WordPress which is on right top.

Click on 1-click WordPressl to install WordPress. 3

To start WordPress instalation on your one.com server - click on new installation.

Click on New Installation. to start installation. 4

Select language and click on install.

Click on Next 5

You will be then redirected to your domain name (your hosted WordPress website). You will see a new screen with a message “Welcome to WordPress”.

Click on Continue 6

Fill the information as asked by the WordPress.

  1. Your username must not contain any capital letter and blank space.
  2. Enter a new password for your WordPress website. It must be very complex so that no one can hack into your system.
  3. Then write your email address, in case you forget your password. This email address will then be used to retrieve or rest your password.
Verify your details and click on Continue 7
  1. Enter the title of your website. Such as “your business name”. Then enter your business tag line. If you don’t have any than just leave this field as it is.
  2. Don’t check on search engine visibility option.
  3. Check on terms and condition to agree and continue.
Verify your details and click on Continue 8

Now you see theme selection page. Don’t select any theme, unless you know how to use them. At the bottom of the page you will find skip to dashboard button.

Click on the skip to dashboard to complete WordPress installation option. 9

You will now see WordPress login option. You don’t need to login now, as you will learn more on next topic, unless you are already familiar with WordPress.

That’s it all done!
