Managing Widgets

Before starting, I recommend you to open your WordPress website. And login to your wp-admin “dashboard of your WordPress website”.

Adding Widgets

1

Widgets can be found on Appearance » Widgets page in your WordPress admin area.

Appearance » Widgets 2

You will see the list of default available widgets in the left hand side of the main work area. On your right hand, you will find the designated areas “Widget-ready areas” on your WordPress site where you can place these widgets.

Simply drag and drop the available widgets to your widget-ready areas. You can also click on a widget title from the list of available widget. WordPress will show you the list of widget-ready areas where you can add this widget. 3

When you add a widget to your widget-ready area, it expands and you can customize its settings and options. Few of them allows you to enter required data in form of text, image or link.

Click on the widget title to expand and customize according to your need. Preview it on your frontend of the website.

Removing Widgets

1

Look for the widget that you want to remove in your widget-ready areas.

Click on the widget title to expand it. Below the widget settings, you will find the link to delete the widget. 2

Caution: Deleting a widget will remove it from your widget-ready areas. Including its settings and content.

Click on the delete link or remove widget link to remove the widget. If you would like to remove a widget without losing the settings, then you need to drag and drop it to the inactive widgets section as you can see below. That’s it all done!
