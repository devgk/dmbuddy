Managing WordPress Category and Tags

In this HOT section I will explain you how to manage WordPress Categories and Tags. Categories and Tags are used to arrange the post in your WordPress website or blog.

Manage WordPress category

Before starting, I recommend you to open your WordPress website. And visit wp-admin “dashboard of your WordPress website”. Login to your WordPress dashboard and follow the instructions.

1

Adding a New Category

If you don’t have any categories yet, follow these steps to add new category.

Go to Posts » All Posts

2
  1. Open the post you want to categories or create a new post.
  2. On post editing page » Click the + Add new category link.
  3. Enter new category name and click “Add new Category”. You’ll then see your new category has been added to the list of categories.
3

To assign a category to a post, click the checkbox next to the category.

4

Manage category

To manage post categories, follow these steps to add manage category.

  1. Go to Posts » Categories
  2. On categories page you can see all categories in the right side.
  3. You can edit, add or delete categories.

Manage WordPress Tag

1

Adding a New Tag

If you don’t have any tags yet, follow these steps to add new tags.

Go to Posts » All Posts

2
  1. Open the post you want to tag or create a new post.
  2. On post editing page » find tag section and enter your tags separated by commas.
  3. Press enter to tag the post.
3

Update tag

  1. On post editing page » find tag section and enter your tags separated by commas as you did before.
  2. Press enter and then you will see your new tags has been added to the list of tags.
  3. Click on cross before tag name to remove tag from the list of tags.
3

Managing tags

To manage post tags, follow these steps

  1. Go to Posts » Tags
  2. On Tag page you can see all your tags in the right side.
  3. You can edit, add or delete tags.
That’s it all done!
