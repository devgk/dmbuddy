Creating Business presence on maps

A map is a visual representation of a geographical area. People use maps to find information regarding their locality.

Digital Map

Digital maps can be accessed through digital devices. You can access digital map through your computer, smartphones, tablets etc. It’s very handy and useful. You can search and locate your destination or any point of interest using digital map.

Importance of a Digital Map for a business

User explores and finds nearby business on their digital map. Creating presence on digital map assures your visibility to local peoples who use map.

Digital map is very important for the businesses’ having local physical address. For example Hotels, Restaurants, Retail Shops, Clinics etc.

Digital map is created using Digital mapping. Digital Mapping or Web Mapping Service is provided by several companies. Among them Google Maps is on the top. After that we have Bing Maps, Apple Maps and more.

Introduction to Google Maps

Google Maps is a web mapping service developed by Google. It offers satellite imagery, street maps, 360° panoramic views of streets (Street View), real-time traffic conditions (Google Traffic), and route planning for traveling by foot, car, bicycle (in beta), or public transportation.

Introduction to Bing map

Bing Maps is a web mapping service provided as a part of Microsoft's Bing suite of search engines. It is very similar to Google map. You can commonly find Bing map on windows devices, such as Microsoft’s phones, tablets and pc’s.
