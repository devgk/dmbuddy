Understanding Social Media Presence

Social presence means, business presence on popular social media websites.

Social media websites is a web application that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking.

Technically: Social media are computer-mediated technologies that allow the creating and sharing of information, ideas, career interests and other forms of expression via virtual communities and networks.

Importance of social media

Social media network such as Facebook is very popular. People have been using social media channels since more than 10 years. There are so many people around us, who use only social media networks. They don’t even know how to browse the internet properly. It’s because they can easily get affordable smartphones with Facebook installed in it. They are so busy with their life and business that they don’t care much about online world. Sometimes they tell their friends and family members to even create their account. So to reach to these people your website won’t going to work! You have to target these users only through social media platforms.

Different social media networks

There are different social media networks all across the internet. But only handful of them are most trusted, popular and used.

Most popular social media platforms:

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google plus
  • LinkedIn and more
