Understanding Social Media Presence

Social presence means, business presence on popular social media websites, such as Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, LinkedIn etc.

Social media websites is a web application that enables users to create and share content and to participate in social networking.

Technically: Social media are computer-mediated technologies that allow the creating and sharing of information, ideas, career interests and other forms of expression via virtual communities and networks.

Importance of social media

Social media network such as Facebook is very popular. People have been using social media channels since more than 10 years. There are so many people around us, who use only social media networks. They don’t know how to browse the internet but they know how to use social media. It’s because we can easily get affordable smartphones with Facebook installed in it. We are so busy with our life and business that we don’t care much about the online world. But we still use social media to connect with our friends and families.

So to reach to the majority of the audience. You have to create your presence and run ADs on all major social media platforms.

Different social media networks

There are different social media networks all across the internet. But the only handful of them are most trusted, popular and used.

Most popular social media platforms:

  • Facebook and Instagram
  • Twitter

  • LinkedIn
