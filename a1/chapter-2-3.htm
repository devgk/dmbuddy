Website requirements

Before creating a website for your business. You must understand the basic requirements to host a website.

In order to host a website, you requires following three things:

  1. Domain Name
  2. Web Hosting and
  3. Website files

Without these, your website will not be accessible online. For better understanding, I have tried to explain these requirements through a simple example.

Let’s consider your website as a real world office premises.

To set up an office. The first thing you need to do, is to purchase the address for your office building. Your office address in this example can be represented as a domain name.

Your office address will be useless, if you don’t construct the office building. Creating infrastructure is a second step to set up an office. Your office building (infrastructure) in this example can be represented as a web hosting server.

Now after constructing an office building. The last thing we require is furniture and equipment. The empty office building can’t be used for any purpose. In this example, furniture and equipment are like your website and its related files.
