Importance of Digital Marketing

In today’s generation, most of the people usually spend a lot of time on the internet. For them, internet is one of their favorite place to find the information they are looking for, search products they need to buy, get-together with a friend or family who are far way, remain connected with the loved ones, play games, watch movies, listen to songs and the list goes on.

A lot of companies and businesses are targeting these online users for their business promotion. For that, they run digital marketing campaigns on social media networks, famous websites, blogs, gaming and entertainment platforms etc. Today many businesses are implementing digital marketing strategies to target online users and to turn them into their client.

Due to affordable digital devices and wider connectivity of the internet. There is an exponential rise in the number of internet users. So to reach to the maximum number of audience, marketers are now following digital marketing practices.

Digital marketing is now considered the 6th mass media channel after print, recordings, cinema, radio and TV.

How Digital Marketing can help you in your business?

The main purpose of digital marketing for any business is to connect with the large section of audience, who are active on the internet, in order to generate business. Among them, digital marketers can easily find potential customers for their business. They target them on their digital devices and promote their product and services according to their preferences and interest.

Suppose I sell designer t-shirts. Now to promote my business I can target online users who regularly visits fashion related websites. I can run advertisements over fashion related websites including social media networks such as Facebook to target them as they might be interested in my designer t-shirts. If you are running a business and getting businesses from newspaper, flyers and other traditional methods. Then you must consider implementing digital marketing techniques in your business in order to exponentially grow your business.

Importance of digital marketing

I have elaborated few points below, which can strengthen the importance of digital marketing.

  1. Wider Reach: As you already knows, the internet has no limits. Using the internet we can reach to each to every corner of the world. The Internet is an essence of Digital marketing. Internet marketing gives you the power to turn a small company into a worldwide famous brand.
  2. Cost-Effective: As I said before, digital marketing is cost effective. Most of its strategies are free or require comparatively low investments. Running advertisements on digital media channels is significantly cheaper than running advertisements on traditional marketing channels such as TV, Newspaper etc.
    Small businesses have very little resources and even capitalization. For them, digital marketing is the best and most cost-effective marketing channel that delivers results in today’s market.
  3. Effective Measuring: Measuring marketing objectives and efforts, on traditional marketing channels is really a difficult task and sometimes it's impossible. On another hand, digital marketing allows us to completely measure each and every activity related to our digital marketing campaign. It allows to effectively manage and improve our campaigns in order to generate more business. Measuring the effectiveness of digital campaigns is an integral part of engaging, exciting and efficient marketing.
  4. Better Targeting: There are no other marketing channels, which allows you to target your audience effectively than digital marketing. Identifying the potential customers and targeting them, can save a lot of marketing resources including time and money. It also helps to increase the chances of conversion. We can target audience based on their interest, locations, languages etc.
  5. Accurate Tracking: Through digital marketing, it is possible to understand your audiences and customers before it actually becomes your client. We can effectively track the activity of your website’s visitor using digital marketing tools. They use digital devices, which we can easily track and monitor. It helps to improve and optimize our digital marketing campaign accordingly.
  6. Interaction: One of the reasons why digital marketing is taking over traditional marketing channels is the ability of Internet marketing tools to interact with targeted audiences in real time. Engagement in any form is what your customers expect to receive when interacting with your brand or business.
  7. Remarketing: Remarketing reduces the risk of losing a customer. We will discuss this in details later. For now, just remember remarketing is a practice of targeting your users who have previously visited your business and to show relevant offers according to their interest.

Digital marketing is applicable for all types of business. It is the best way to reach to the widest majority of audience easily. Implementing the digital marketing strategies which you will learn in this course will always be beneficial for your business.
